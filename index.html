Green EDP

GreenEDP is a living laboratory where undergraduate, graduate, and professional students engage with staff and faculty to pilot new eco-driven solar technologies and policies for the Emergent Digital Practices program at the University of Denver. GreenEDP hopes to help the University of Denver become a leader in sustainability and build on its reputation as a progressive, forward thinking institution. My collaborators and I worked on this project throughout the last half of the Spring 2015 quarter at the University of Denver. I was in charge of managing some of the content for the web-page, including taking and editing the headshots of the collaborators and designing the logo on the left.

Denver GEARS

Designed in Spring 2013 as a visually interactive mapping resource, GEARS aims to increase bike commuting in the Denver Metro area. Cradle to Cradle

This is a short piece I created demonstrating the possibility of a cigarette butt that can biodegrade without leeching harmful chemicals into the environment. The goal of the project was to create a video piece that demonstrated a possible cradle to cradle scenario for an everyday object. We were required to use both original and attributed footage in this project. I shot the original footage with my DSLR camera and edited it with Final Cut Pro.

Multiples

For this project I wanted to explore things that were familiar and interesting to me. I love taking photographs, especially of landscapes. However, I wanted to show different kinds of landscapes. This short video piece focuses on the juxtaposition of urban versus natural landscapes. I filmed in locations that best exemplified these types of landscapes, All while keeping a photograph approach to shooting. As for the cinematic aspect of the piece, I wanted to get footage that was not only visually interesting in its content, but in its process as well. I used pans, tilts, and pull-focus shots to enhance transitions. The piece was created for a project during a class on Time-based media, and required a dichotomy be made through the content of the video.
